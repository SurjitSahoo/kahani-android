Kahani (meaning "story") is a modern, offline-first client for Audiobookshelf, designed specifically for a premium audiobook and podcast experience on Android.

Built with Material Design 3 and full Dynamic Color support, Kahani feels like a native part of your device while providing powerful features for power listeners.

✨ KEY FEATURES

• Offline-First: Download entire books or specific chapters to listen anywhere, anytime.
• Seamless Sync: Your listening progress syncs automatically across all your devices and the server.
• Visual Excellence: Stunning Material You interface with smooth transitions and adaptive themes.
• Advanced Playback: Variable playback speed, skip silence, and customizable seek intervals.
• Chapter Support: Easily browse and navigate through chapters with precise progress tracking.
• Podcast Ready: Full support for both your audiobook collection and your favorite podcasts.
• Sleep Timer: Fall asleep without worry using the flexible sleep timer (time-based or end-of-chapter).
• Home Widget: Control your current story directly from your home screen.
• Network Aware: Intelligently manages offline/online states and server availability.

Kahani is focused on simplicity, privacy, and the joy of a good story. 

Note: An Audiobookshelf server instance is required to use this application.
